Our Shop

“Blütezeit” was founded in 2010 and is located in the heart of Salzburg in Austria.

Our business specializes in intricately handmade floral arrangements. From individual orders to event commissions - our team will happily guide you and offer expertise along the way to help you reach your goal.

With our craft, we would love to help out in bringing more bloom into your life!
